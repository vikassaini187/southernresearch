Internal Communications

Southern Research strives to ensure that every employee is kept informed of Southern Research matters that affect them. Leaders are encouraged to engage employees in conversation about topical issues in staff meetings. Southern Research strives to communicate with all employees through a variety of media including: an employee Intranet, leadership commentary publication, employee e-mail, and bulletin boards for the dissemination of information.
